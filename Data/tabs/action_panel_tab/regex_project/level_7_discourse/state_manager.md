# Interaction State Tracking (Level 7)

- [ ] **Speaker ID:** Who is speaking?
- [ ] **Current Domain:** (Academic, Informal, Technical, etc.)
- [ ] **Active Intent:** What is the most recently detected goal?
- [ ] **Entity Queue:** What objects/times/people were mentioned?
- [ ] **Sentiment Buffer:** What is the current emotional tone of the user?
- [ ] **Turn Count:** How many exchanges have occurred?
- [ ] **Pending Requests:** Are there tasks waiting for confirmation?
- [ ] **Anaphora Resolution:** What does "it" or "that" refer to in the current context?
